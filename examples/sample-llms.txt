# example.com

> Example.com is a B2B observability platform that helps engineering teams find and fix production issues 10x faster through unified logs, traces, and metrics.

## Product

- [Pricing](https://example.com/pricing): Plans start at $99/mo for 10 users with 30-day retention. Enterprise tier includes SSO, audit logs, and dedicated support.
- [Features](https://example.com/features): Unified observability across logs, metrics, distributed traces, and front-end RUM in a single timeline view.
- [Integrations](https://example.com/integrations): 87 native integrations including AWS, GCP, Kubernetes, Datadog import, PagerDuty, Slack, GitHub.
- [Security](https://example.com/security): SOC 2 Type II, ISO 27001, HIPAA-ready, EU data residency, customer-managed encryption keys.

## Docs

- [Getting Started](https://example.com/docs/getting-started): Install the agent, send your first event, and build your first dashboard in under 10 minutes.
